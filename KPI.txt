Below is a detailed KPI appraisal framework for Adarsh Shipping and Services for freight forwarding, customs clearance, HR, accounts, and admin teams. 

I have designed it so you can directly convert this into your appraisal system or Excel sheet. 

KPIs should be measurable and linked to business goals. In freight forwarding and logistics, common measurable KPIs include on-time delivery, transit-time variance, shipment exceptions, cost/margin per shipment, documentation accuracy, and customer updates. Customs-clearance performance is commonly measured through clearance time, first-time clearance, error rate, and compliance accuracy. (Investopedia)
