Project Overview & Objectives
What AMS is, why it exists, and the business goals it supports
What the system does
Digitizes the full appraisal cycle from initiation and self-assessment through reviewer ratings, management decision, salary revision, meeting records, and closure.
Problems Solved
Replaces paper forms, spreadsheets, and email chasing with a structured, transparent workflow. Fixes inconsistent ratings, un-auditable decisions, and missed deadlines.
Core Objectives
  • Streamline workflow: Define a strict digital appraisal lifecycle.
  • Fair Scoring: Ensure scores via three independent reviewers.
  • Automate Triggers: Deadlines, reminders, and arrear calculations.
  • Protect Data: Role-based access and complete audit history.
  • Centralize Records: Ratings, salary revisions, MOMs, and KPIs in one place.

System Capabilities
Core appraisal processes combined into one unified view
Digital Assessments
Employees complete a 9-category self-assessment. Three reviewers then score independently before scores are revealed.
KPI Tracking
Monthly KPI history connects day-to-day employee performance directly with year-end appraisal decisions.
Salary & Arrears
Management selects increment slabs. Late meetings trigger automatic daily-rate arrear calculation for approval.
Meeting Minutes
HR, Admin, and Management can record and store separate meeting notes (MOM) against each appraisal cycle.
User Roles & Access Control
Seven roles with strict access boundaries and clear responsibilities
Admin
Full system control, cycle creation, salary slabs, extensions, and data tools.
Management
Reviews aggregate ratings, decides salary hikes, approves arrears.
HR
Reviewer role, confirms availability, writes MOM, handles notifications.
Team Lead
Rates direct reports, confirms availability, views team KPI data, votes on dates.
Manager
Independent reviewer for assigned employees with rating responsibilities.
Employee
Performs self-assessment, views cycle status, KPI records, and salary history.
Partner/Director
Read-only oversight across all employee records and appraisal information.
Users can hold dual roles (e.g., HR + Employee) so one person can submit their own appraisal while reviewing others.

Automation & Triggers
The system keeps users moving without manual email chasing
Automated Triggers
  • Anniversary Detection: Detects appraisal month and notifies Admin to create a cycle.
  • Smart Unlocking: Self-assessment is unlocked automatically as soon as reviewers confirm availability.
  • Deadline Reminders: Sent before self-assessment and rating deadlines close.
  • Arrear Auto-Calculation: Late meetings automatically create daily-rate arrears for payout approval.

Governance & Security
Controls and records combined for auditability and management oversight
Security Controls
  • Authentication: Challenge-based passkey login, reset approval, and bcrypt hashing.
  • Role-Based Access: Every page enforces permissions; salary data is visible only where required.
  • Session Management: Timeout controls, active session monitoring, and admin forced logout.
  • Privacy Controls: Employees cannot view others' data; partner access is strictly read-only.
Reports & Audit Trails
  • Complete Audit Trail: Actions, ratings, decisions, state changes, and timestamps are permanently logged.
  • Appraisal History: Ratings, reviewers, meeting dates, and MOM records per employee.
  • Salary Sheet: Components, approved revisions, effective dates, and payout months.
  • Security Logs: All login attempts and reset requests are available for admin review.
